Questions

Things people ask before uploading.

Is Healthie a substitute for my doctor?

No. Healthie helps you understand your documents, but it does not replace medical or financial professionals. Always confirm findings with a licensed clinician.

What file types can I upload?

PDFs and photos of documents: lab results, visit notes, imaging reports, bills, EOBs, and MyChart exports.

Can Healthie diagnose me?

No. Healthie explains what a document says and flags things worth asking about. It never diagnoses or recommends treatment.

What is BYOK, and do I need it?

BYOK means connecting your own Gemini API key. It is optional. The hosted option works fully out of the box, and BYOK just lowers cost at higher usage.

How is my data kept private?

Documents are encrypted at rest and in transit, stored with signed access URLs, and never used to train models without your consent.
